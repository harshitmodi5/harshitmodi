About TicTacToe

  • TicTacToe is an iOS and android native application that is a game which we used to play on a paper can now be played on a digital platform.
  • Many brands and companies have established franchises in various regions of the world in recent years to expand their business, but there is a lack of a reliable platform where shop owners can communicate with franchise owners.
  • This application will let store owners search different franchise and get full information on franchises and allow them to submit a proposal request for a franchise.
  • User can store game data and check later who won the game.
  • User can re-play the whole game and track back to the steps played before.
